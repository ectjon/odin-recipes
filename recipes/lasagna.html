Lasagna with Meat Sauce

Description

This lasagna recipe uses a bechemel sauce along with ragu to create a creamy, rich lasagna. It is great for a weeknight dinner due to its simple preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Flour
  • 3 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Parmesean Cheese
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the pasta sheets according to directions on box
  2. Chop the onion. Saute the chopped onion in a large pot with the olive oil
  3. After the onion has browned, about 10 minutes, add ground beef to the pot
  4. Once the beef is fully cooked, add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer
  5. Meanwhile, start the bechemel sauce by adding the butter to a small pan
  6. Once the butter is melted, stir in the flour. Cook until the flour is no longer raw, about 3 minutes
  7. Whisk in the milk into the flour mixture until smooth and bring to a simmer. Season with salt and remove from heat.
  8. Layer the pasta sheets, meat sauce, bechemel sauce, and cheese in a baking pan. Repeat for 2 or 3 more layers.
  9. Bake the lasagna at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 1 hour. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
