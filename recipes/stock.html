Roasted Chicken Stock

Description

This chicken stock recipe is a great way to use the leftover meat and bones from Roast Chicken with Potatoes. The onion, carrots, and ginger help give the broth depth of flavor

Ingredients

  • 1 Roast Chicken Carass
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Thumb-sized piece of Ginger
  • 1 Bay Leaf

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the carrot. Dice the oninon
    2. Add all ingredients to large stock pot with 4 quarts of water. Bring to a boil.
  2. Simmer for 2 hours. Strain the liquid and drink immediately or place in fridge when cool.
